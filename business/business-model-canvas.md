# Business Model Canvas for medidata-vault

## Customer Segments
- Healthcare providers (hospitals, clinics) managing patient data.
- Medical researchers needing access to large datasets for studies.
- Health tech startups developing applications requiring medical data.
- Pharmaceutical companies conducting clinical trials and drug development.

## Value Propositions
- Secure, HIPAA-compliant platform ensuring data privacy and security.
- Built-in compliance checks to streamline regulatory adherence.
- Advanced analytics and visualization tools for better data insights.
- Version control for datasets, allowing tracking of changes over time.

## Channels
- Direct sales to healthcare organizations and research institutions.
- Partnerships with health tech accelerators and incubators.
- Online marketing through industry-specific platforms and forums.
- Webinars and workshops demonstrating platform capabilities.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated account managers for enterprise clients to ensure support.
- Online community forum for user engagement and feedback.
- Regular updates and newsletters about new features and compliance changes.
- Training sessions and resources for onboarding new users.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: $500/month (approx. 17,500 THB) for small organizations.
- Tiered pricing for larger organizations based on data volume and user count.
- Additional fees for premium features (e.g., advanced analytics).
- Consulting services for compliance and data management best practices.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in data security and compliance.
- Infrastructure for secure data storage and processing.
- Partnerships with compliance experts and legal advisors.
- Marketing and sales team with industry expertise.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and enhancement of the platform.
- Regular audits and updates to maintain HIPAA compliance.
- Customer support and training to ensure user satisfaction.
- Market research to identify emerging needs and trends in medical data management.

## Key Partners
- Compliance consulting firms for regulatory guidance.
- Cloud service providers for secure data hosting.
- Health tech associations for networking and outreach.
- Research institutions for collaboration on data-driven projects.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: salaries for engineers and compliance experts.
- Marketing expenses for outreach and customer acquisition.
- Infrastructure costs for cloud services and data storage.
- Customer support and training resources.
